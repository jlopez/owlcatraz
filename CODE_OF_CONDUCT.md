# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][covenant], which
is the de-facto standard code of conduct for open-source projects. The full
text (including the standards of expected behavior, enforcement guidelines,
and reporting procedures) is published at the link above and is incorporated
here by reference.

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, focus on the work, and keep
discussions on-topic. Owlcatraz is a small utility maintained in spare time,
and the contributor community should reflect that — collaborative, technical,
and welcoming to newcomers.

## Reporting

To report a code-of-conduct concern, email **owlcatraz@jesusla.com**. Reports
will be reviewed and handled confidentially. The reporter's identity will
not be shared without their consent.

For security-vulnerability reports specifically (which are not
code-of-conduct concerns), see [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) instead.

[covenant]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
